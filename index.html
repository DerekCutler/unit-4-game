Crystals Collector



Instructions:


  • You will be given a random number at the start of the game
  • There are four crystals above. By clicking on a crystal you will a specific amount of points to your total score.
  • You win the game by matching your score to the random number provided. However, if you go over the random number; you lose.
  • The value of the crystal is hidden from you to spice up the game.
  • Each time you restart the game, the value of the crystal will also change.
  • May the force be ever in your favor, always!
